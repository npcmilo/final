Impact of Viral Videos

As of December 10th, the @fortytwobts TikTok account has 9,378 followers. We found that 92% of our followers on TikTok directly trace back to our viral videos,
that is, videos that received over 100k+ views. This shows the importance of appealing to the TikTok algorithm when aiming to grow our brand audience. 47% of
online sessions stem from social channels, so scaling our online following is required to grow the company.

Conclusion



It is clear that in order to scale, FORTYTWO must increase marketing spend to tap into budding markets. Presence within the US is strong, with international
interest looming. High volumes of online store sessions can be attributed to virality of short-form content, with conversion rates looking to improve. The
emerging markets of Florida, Illinois, and England show signs of upside potential due to their relatively low conversion rates and high session volume. This
may indicate that customers are becoming familiar with the company and its products in these regions, but have not yet been exposed to quality content long
enough to complete a purchase. As seen in the final visualization, driving traffic from these markets towards the highest-quality content released by FORTYTWO
may have a significant positive impact on increasing gross revenue.

However, it is important to explore the return on investments of emerging markets (Illinois, Florida, England) to popular markets (Califonia, New York, New Jersey)
when allocating resources towards paid advertising.
